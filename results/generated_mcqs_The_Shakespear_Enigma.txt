## MCQ 1
Question: What unusual characteristic did the fruits of the Shakespear tree possess?
A) They were shaped like apples.
B) They shimmered with a golden hue.
C) They tasted like pineapples.
D) They emitted a sweet fragrance.
Correct Answer: B

## MCQ 2
Question: What did Emma dream of becoming?
A) A painter
B) A musician
C) A writer
D) A dancer
Correct Answer: C

## MCQ 3
Question: What effect did tasting the Shakespear fruit have on Emma?
A) She gained the ability to predict the weather.
B) She experienced visions of her future.
C) She developed a fear of heights.
D) She lost her ability to speak.
Correct Answer: B
